About Protosahan

As part of the social responsibility to nurture and encourage school students by providing them with an opportunity of a platform to showcase the knowledge and talents they have amassed over the years. To commit to the motive, and encourage a mass of students Protsahan has been organised to impart the value-based knowledge to the school students in their formative years and help them light the zeal of learning in their hearts. Previous editions have reached out to more than 30,000 students from 300+ schools of Jaipur and nearby areas so far.

Table Tennis

Table tennis is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball, also known as the ping-pong ball, back and forth across a table using small solid rackets. The game takes place on a hard table divided by a net. Team composition: Members: 1player Rules and regulations: Game is played to 11 points. Players serve two serves each,alternating. A player does not have to win specifically off their own serve in order to win a point. If a game ties at 10-10, a player must win by 2 points. In this situation, players serve one serve each, alternating. You must throw the ball up straight, from a flat palm, at least 6inches (16cm). Your toss and service contact must be behind the table surface (not over). If the ball hits the net during service, it is a let, the point is replayed. There is no limit or point deductions for let serves. Proper decorum is mandatory and judges decision is final.
